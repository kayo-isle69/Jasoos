How to Play
Simple by design.
بسيط من حيث التصميم
01
SETUP THE GAME

Choose player count, number of spies, question rounds, and a word category.

اختر عدد اللاعبين والجواسيس والفئة
02
PEEK YOUR ROLE

Each player holds & lifts the card to peek their role — spy or player. Release to hide it.

كل لاعب يرفع البطاقة ليرى دوره ثم يتركها
03
QUESTION ROUNDS

Players ask each other about the secret word. Spies must bluff without knowing the word.

يسأل اللاعبون بعضهم عن الكلمة السرية
04
VOTE & ELIMINATE

Everyone votes on who they think is the spy. Most votes = eliminated.

يصوّت الجميع على من يظنونه الجاسوس
05
LAST CHANCE

Caught spies get one final shot: guess the secret word to still win.

الجاسوس المكشوف يحصل على فرصة أخيرة لتخمين الكلمة
Features
Everything you need.
كل ما تحتاجه
🎴
DRAG-TO-PEEK
A satisfying card mechanic — drag up to reveal your role, release to hide it. No tapping through awkward menus.
🌍
BILINGUAL
Full Arabic and English support, RTL layout included. Switch languages at any time from the main menu.
📱
OFFLINE READY
Install as a PWA and play anywhere — no internet needed. Everything runs on-device.
🕵️
MULTIPLE SPIES
Configure 1 or more spies for a harder game. Balance the tension against your group size.
🌙
DARK / LIGHT MODE
Toggle between a dark spy aesthetic and a bright daytime mode directly from the main menu.
🎲
12 CATEGORIES
From food and animals to movies and video games — pick a category or let it randomize.
